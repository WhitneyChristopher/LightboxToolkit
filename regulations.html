Regulations and Project Proposals

Certain constraints apply to the physical space of the Lightbox.

Cords cannot be in the path to the exits.

Cords elsewhere on the floor must be taped down. Contact Collections Management or Facilities to coordinate taping the cords down.

Any materials brought into the space (e.g., fabric screens) need to be fire rated.

Waivers are required for bringing art components in and out of the gallery and building. Speak to Collections Management about this process.

If you intend to bring any additional equipment or components into the Lightbox, it would be useful to draw out on the Lightbox floorplan where you intend to place each object, its measurements and materials, how you intend to mount or affix it, where cords and other components will lead, and any other relevant details. Please bring this to any proposal and project review meetings.
