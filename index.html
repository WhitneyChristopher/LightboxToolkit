Lightbox Toolkit

This toolkit provides information on, and specifications about, the Lightbox Gallery and its equipment.



Specification notes:

The wall of screens on the east side of the Lightbox Gallery...
- Is 142.50 inches wide and 80.25 inches tall. (362 x 204 cm)
- (Each individual screen is 47.50 inches wide and 26.75 inches tall. (120.65 x 68.00 cm))
- Has a resolution, all the screens together, of 5760 by 3240.
- Has four speakers, as well as wireless microphones.

The projection onto the wall of shades on the west side of the Lightbox Gallery...
- Is 240.0 inches wide and 67.5 inches tall. (610.0 x 171.5 cm)
- Has a resolution of 3840 x 1080.

Both run Windows 7, and come installed with Kiosk Launcher (which opens a full screen kiosk mode IE 11 web browser), Processing 2, Processing 3, and VLC. Note that the taskbar can also be hidden with Ctrl+Esc.

Available controllers for interaction include a variety of remotes, keyboards, joysticks, and mice.

Video is processed by two Vista Spyder X20s, and controlled with a Crestron Pro-3.

Two floor boxes also contain a variety of power, network, and utility lines.
